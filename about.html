About Us

We make brands feel local—and loved.

India’s audiences are diverse. We partner with creators who speak their language and share their culture.

Our Vision

Founded to bridge the gap between national brands and regional communities, ReachUp Media curates micro and nano creators who inspire real action. We combine cultural intelligence with creator relationships to deliver campaigns that feel native, not intrusive.
